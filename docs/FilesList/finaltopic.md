## 3. SDGs Analysis

<div class="center">
    <img src="https://www.lubrizol.com/-/media/Lubrizol/Personal-Care/Images/LH/Sustainable-Development-Goals.png" style="width:75%;">
</div>

Our project addresses multiple Sustainable Development Goals (SDGs) and focuses primarily on the following aspects:

3.1 **Quality Education**: The digital restoration of Sichuan Opera face-changing and the design of digital facial patterns contribute to a better understanding of intangible cultural heritage. The interactive installation allows audiences to learn and experience the skills and emotional expressions of Sichuan Opera face-changing more deeply.

3.2 **Sustainable Cities and Communities**: The project emphasizes intangible cultural heritage and seeks to protect and pass on traditional elements in an innovative way. Through digital technology, emotional expressions in facial patterns can be widely disseminated to new audiences, promoting the inheritance of intangible cultural heritage and creating richer, more interactive cultural experiences in cities and communities.

3.3 **Industry, Innovation, and Infrastructure**: The research on achieving facial pattern interactivity through digital technology not only explores new forms of intangible cultural heritage expression but also adds innovative elements to traditional art. This contributes to industrial upgrades and fosters innovation in the preservation of intangible cultural heritage.

3.4 **Reduced Inequality**: By digitally representing facial pattern emotions, the project helps promote traditional culture in a more attractive way, reducing inequality in cultural heritage transmission. It enables a broader audience to participate in and enjoy the pleasures of traditional culture.

3.5 **Responsible Consumption and Production**: The project transforms intangible cultural heritage into interactive theatrical facial patterns using digital technology, providing audiences with a richer cultural experience. This helps broaden the innovation and transformation of intangible cultural heritage into marketable products, encouraging responsible cultural production and consumption.

In summary, our project is closely aligned with various SDGs, fostering the inheritance, innovation, and sustainable development of intangible cultural heritage through the innovative application of digital technology.
